GIAIC

GOVERNOR INITIATIVE FOR ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND COMPUTING

The Governor Initiative for Artificial Intelligence and Computing (GIAIC) is a privately funded not-for-profit educational initiative started by Sir Zia khan, Kamran Khan Tessori, Daniyal Nagori, Ameen Alam to help promote education, research and business opporrtunities in emerging teach fields like

  • Cloud Native Computing
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Blockchain
  • Internet Of Thing

Cloud Native Computing

The Cloud Native Computing program prepares students to program in open-source technologies like Linux, Docker, Kubernetes, JavaScript, Node.js, React, and React Native. It introduces them to the development of Microservices and software development techniques on cloud platforms like AWS.

Artificial Intelligence

The Artificial Intelligence program takes students through the foundation of AI technology. It involves programming in open-source languages such as Python, Numpy, Pandas, Tensorflow 2.0, and Flask, program covers deploying practical AI solutions and use cases, and provides real-life examples of modern-day AI in practice.

Block Chain

The Blockchain program emphasizes understanding blockchains such as Ethereum and Hyperledger (Fabric), Students learn to program in languages such as Solidity and JavaScript on frameworks like Node.js. They are introduced to the development of Distributed Apps (DApps) using frameworks like Web3 and Truffle, along with Blockchain-as-a-service (BaaS) and Initial Coin Offering (ICO) of crypto-tokens.

Internet of Thing

The Internet of Things program introduces students to open-source development technologies likeRust, Embedded Rust, WebAssembly, Hyper, Diesel, Google Assistant, and Kubernetes The program makes them proficient in developing Microservices for IoT Edge, IoT Cloud, and voice computing for device control.
